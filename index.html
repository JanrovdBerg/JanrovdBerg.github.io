About Me

Hello! I'm Janro van der Berg, a Mechanical Engineer with experience in Semiconductor Lithography Equipment.
I was born and raised in Nelspruit, South Africa, where I developed a strong work ethic and a passion for problem-solving.

Growing up in a boys' hostel, I quickly learned the importance of discipline and responsibility.
I also had the privilege of spending time on my family’s farms, which became my favorite place to be.
Life on the farm taught me practical skills, resilience, and the value of maintaining what you build—as we say in South Africa: "A farmer always makes a plan."

Realizing my love for solving complex challenges, I pursued a degree in Mechanical Engineering at North-West University in Potchefstroom.
After graduating, I joined a small but highly skilled engineering team designing and manufacturing Chlorine Dioxide Generators for water purification.
In this role, I gained hands-on experience in product design, procurement, logistics, maintenance, and scaling production.
I also contributed to the design of an improved water-cleaning process.

Wanting to expand my knowledge and push my limits, I moved to the Netherlands and joined ASML as a System Performance Engineer.
This role constantly challenges me with new problems to solve, and I love every moment of it.
I’m passionate about continuous learning, innovation, and making systems work efficiently.
